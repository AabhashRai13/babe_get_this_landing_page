Delete Your Account

Applies to: Babe, Get This (Android) — developer: Aabhash Rai

You can permanently delete your Babe, Get This account at any time — right here, in the app, or by email. We're sad to see you go, but it's your data and your call.

Delete it now

Sign in with your account below and your account is deleted immediately — same as the delete button in the app.

Instant deletion is temporarily unavailable on the website — you can still delete your account from inside the app (Settings → Delete account), or by email below.

Or request deletion by email

  • Send an email to raiaabhash3@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account".
  • Send the email from the email address you registered with, or include that address in the message, so we can verify the request is really yours.
  • We will confirm the request and complete the deletion within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email when it's done.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email address and authentication credentials).
  • Any feedback or support correspondence linked to your account, unless we are legally required to keep it.

What is not affected

  • Your lists — they live only on your device and were never uploaded to our servers. Uninstall the app or clear its storage in Android settings to remove them.
  • Lists you shared as text — copies you sent to others through messaging apps live in those apps and can't be recalled by us.
  • Legal retention — in rare cases we may need to retain minimal records where required by law; these are deleted as soon as that requirement ends.
Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you just want a fresh start, you can also simply delete individual lists inside the app and keep your account.

Questions?

Email us at raiaabhash3@gmail.com — happy to help. You can also read our full Privacy Policy to see exactly what data the app collects.
